Hello, welcome to my website!

This website will chronicle my two-and-a-half part senior capstone project. The two-and-a-half parts are as follows:

  • Creating this very website. This might look somewhat blog-like, and it might not; time will tell. Since the primary point of this website is to record my senior project, I only consider it to be half of a part.
  • Building and learning to operate a moderate-sized (I'm currently thinking about 4'x3') modular CNC machine in my basement. This would primarily be used as a router for wood or thin sheet aluminum, but ideally, I would also be able to swap in other tools at a later date.
  • Finally, designing and building (using in part the new, hopefully-functional by now CNC) some large project with a lot of "wow factor." I'm currently thinking a trebuchet, but, since this will be the last part of my project, it's very much subject to change. My ideal plan would be to try mounting the trebuchet it on a turntable and try to get it to auto-target April Tags or similar things.

Since this website is very much under construction, expect it to change a lot if you come back to it later. I'll try to keep most of the content, but no guarantees.
